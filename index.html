ABOUT THE LEAGUE

Football, or soccer, is a dynamic team sport played worldwide. Two teams compete to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing net, using only their feet, heads, and torsos. Originating in 19th-century England, it has grown into a global phenomenon, highlighted by events like the FIFA World Cup. Renowned for its simplicity and inclusivity, football fosters community, athleticism, and national pride, making it "the beautiful game" for millions.
